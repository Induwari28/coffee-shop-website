Our History

NesLeo Coffee Shop was founded with a simple mission: to serve high-quality coffee made from carefully selected beans.

Our beans are sourced from trusted farms and roasted to bring out their rich aroma and unique flavor. Every cup we serve is prepared with care by our skilled baristas.

Whether you're looking for a quick morning coffee or a relaxing afternoon break, our shop offers a cozy space and unforgettable taste.
